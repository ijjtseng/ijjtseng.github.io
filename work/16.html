CNN Homepage
& Fact Check Visuals

Web Product, HTML/CSS, Javascript

I had been working on ways to show stories on homepage as a pet project and it came to life on two main occasions — State of the Union fact check evening and President Trump's speech on declaring a national emergency on the US southern border.

Around the same time, I became involved in fact check stories (known as Facts First). Designed and developed fact check story layouts.
