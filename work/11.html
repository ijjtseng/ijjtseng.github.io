Presidential
Speech Library

Sketch, InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop

The old presidential library was last updated when Obama was first inaugurated in 2009 and it was running on Flash. This project was completed at the beginning of 2017, with a new president comes a refresh (along with a new inauguration speech).

View the library here.

My inital design pitch to the boss was admittedly not a style typically seen in Politics. I wanted to find a way to branch out and create a more unique and on-trend CNN Politics style. Not that trendiness matters in Politics (or does it?) but most all media companies had very stoic styles for politic-news content. This project helped me inform the Politics rebrand and styleguide.

The first draft combined geometric trends with the previous handdrawn doodle style from 2016 elections and past.

Draft got nixed for something more evergreen and easier to mass produce. While it was important to update the speech library, it wasn't a main page view driver so it didn't make sense to spend an inordinate amount of time on the revamp.
